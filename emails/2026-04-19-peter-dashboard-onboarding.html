Your 4-Step Daily Workflow
  1. Open today's dashboard (the 7-Day Posting Calendar section tells you which one).
  2. Find the format scheduled for today. Click the gold Copy Content button on that panel.
  3. Paste into the destination platform (Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc). Attach the video or image listed on the panel.
  4. Mark that day's post ✓ done in our shared tracker.
About the video formats
I render all the HeyGen avatar videos myself — you never need to touch PowerShell or an API. When you open a video format panel, you'll see one of three cards at the top: yellow (still rendering, don't post yet), green with an embedded video (ready to download and post), or red (render failed, Slack me). That's the whole logic.
One Rule I Want You To Memorize

Never edit the script, caption, or SSML on the dashboard. If you see a typo, Slack me — don't fix it yourself. Reason: the dashboard version is the source of truth. Fixing a typo only in the Instagram post means next week, when I regenerate a similar piece, the typo comes back. Fixing it at the source means it's gone forever.

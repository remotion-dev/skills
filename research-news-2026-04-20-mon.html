Scraper Skill Specification

skills/local-news-scraper/SKILL.md

A skill that pulls local news from configured sources via web search + RSS feeds where available.

Source config:

  • City sites: cityofepa.org, redwoodcity.org, cityofpaloalto.org, menlopark.gov, smcgov.org (Press Releases + City Council minutes)
  • Regional news: SF Chronicle, Mercury News, KTVU, Palo Alto Weekly, The Daily Post, Embarcadero Media
  • Industry: Freddie Mac weekly rate survey, MBA application data, Bankrate daily rate feed
  • Permit trackers: Planning Commission agendas, Zoning Board decisions

Skill uses WebFetch + WebSearch for content, extracts date/headline/summary/URL, writes to outputs/local-news-{date}.json. The Opportunity Score's Timeliness criterion reads this feed — a topic matching a news item from the last 7 days gets Timeliness = 5.

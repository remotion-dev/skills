# 25 ChatGPT Ads Moments for Real Estate

Reference library for selecting and customizing ad group context hints. Each moment includes the persona, intent, ideal landing page, and the original context-hint template from the AiM resource. Replace bracketed placeholders with the user's actual market data from `market-config.md`.

Organized into three sections: Agent Moments (top-of-funnel agent selection), Seller Moments, Buyer Moments.

---

## AGENT MOMENTS

These describe the moment someone is searching for a person, not a property. Highest-intent moments in the library.

### 1. Buyer or seller searching for the best local agent

**Ideal landing page:** About / Bio page. Downshift: Homepage.

**Context hint template:**
> People in [CITY/METRO] who are getting ready to buy or sell a home and trying to figure out which real estate agent to work with. They want to know who the top-performing agents in the area are, who has the best track record in their specific neighborhood or price range, and how to choose someone they can trust with one of the biggest financial decisions they'll make.

**When to recommend this:** User has a strong About page. User wants more general visibility. User does both buyer and seller work.

---

### 2. Luxury buyer wanting access and representation

**Ideal landing page:** Luxury specialization page. Downshift: Buyer Services, then About / Bio.

**Context hint template:**
> Buyers in [CITY/METRO] shopping for a home in the [$X-$Y] price range who want to work with an agent who can give them access to off-market and pre-listing inventory, represent them in private negotiations, and protect their interests in a high-value transaction. They're looking for a local expert with deep relationships at the top of the market and the discretion these deals require.

**When to recommend this:** User does luxury and has confirmed a luxury price threshold in market-config. User has stated luxury as a niche-to-grow.

---

### 3. Luxury seller wanting elevated marketing

**Ideal landing page:** Luxury specialization page. Downshift: Seller Services, then Home Valuation.

**Context hint template:**
> Owners of luxury homes in [CITY/METRO] considering selling their property in the [$X-$Y] price range. They want to understand how high-end listings are marketed, what professional photography and video should look like, how their home will be exposed to qualified buyers locally and globally, and how to find an agent with a proven track record on premium homes.

**When to recommend this:** User does luxury listings. User does seller-side work in the luxury threshold range.

## SELLER MOMENTS

### 4. Seller ready to price the home

**Ideal landing page:** Home Valuation page.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] who are preparing to sell their house and trying to figure out what it's actually worth in today's market. They want a real number based on recent comparable sales nearby, current buyer demand in their neighborhood, and what their home would realistically sell for if they listed in the next few months. They're looking for a local agent to give them an accurate, market-grounded valuation.

**When to recommend this:** User has a Home Valuation page. Almost always a strong pick for any seller-focused agent — high intent, clear conversion path.

---

### 5. First-time seller

**Ideal landing page:** Seller Services page. Downshift: Home Valuation.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] selling a home for the first time. They're trying to understand the whole process from start to finish — how to prepare the home, how pricing actually works, what commission and closing costs look like, what to expect from showings and offers, and how to find an agent who will walk them through it patiently.

**When to recommend this:** User markets as patient/educational. User has a strong Seller Services page that walks through the process.

---

### 6. Frustrated seller whose home isn't selling

**Ideal landing page:** Expired Listing or Marketing specialization page. Downshift: Seller Services.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] whose house has been listed for sale for weeks or months without an offer. They're trying to figure out why their home isn't selling, whether the price is the problem, whether the marketing is working, and what their options are right now to get the house sold — including whether they need a new agent who can market the home differently.

**When to recommend this:** User specializes in expired listings or aggressive marketing. User actively pursues stale listings as a niche.

---

**Context hint template:**

They're trying to figure out what went wrong, what to do differently, how to reprice, and how to find a new agent with a different marketing approach who can get the home sold in the next few months.

**When to recommend this:** User has expired-listing as an active niche. User has a dedicated relist or expired specialization page.

---

They're running into problems with pricing, marketing exposure, showing logistics, negotiating offers, or handling the paperwork, and they're reconsidering whether selling on their own is actually worth it — and what an experienced agent could do for them instead.

**When to recommend this:** User specializes in FSBO conversion. User has FSBO as a niche-to-grow.

### 9. Seller deciding when to list

**Ideal landing page:** Home Valuation page. Downshift: Seller Services.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] who know they want to sell in the next year but are trying to figure out when to actually list. They're watching mortgage rates, local inventory, and seasonal patterns, and they want a local agent to walk them through what the market is doing right now and when the right window is for their specific home and neighborhood.

**When to recommend this:** User markets as a market-timing expert. User wants top-of-funnel seller leads further out from listing.

---

### 10. Seller-then-buyer (selling and buying together)

**Ideal landing page:** Move-up or Seller-then-Buyer page. Downshift: Seller Services.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] who need to sell their current house and buy their next one at the same time. They're trying to figure out how to time the two transactions, whether to sell first or buy first, how to handle the gap, and how to make a competitive offer on their next home while their current one is still on the market. They need a local agent who can coordinate both sides.

**When to recommend this:** User does both sides. User markets as a coordination specialist.

---

### 11. Rightsizing into a better-fit home

**Ideal landing page:** Rightsizing specialization page. Downshift: Seller Services.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] thinking about moving into a home that better fits their current lifestyle — less square footage and easier maintenance, a different floor plan, a different location, or features their current home doesn't have. They want a local agent who can help them figure out the right move, coordinate the sale of their current home with the purchase of the next one, and identify homes that fit what they actually want now.

**When to recommend this:** User has rightsizing as a niche. Note: avoid framing tied to age — focus on lifestyle and fit, not "downsizing for retirees."

---

### 12. Seller in financial distress

**Ideal landing page:** Short Sale or Pre-Foreclosure specialization page. Downshift: Seller Services.

**Context hint template:**
> Homeowners in [CITY/METRO] who are behind on their mortgage or facing financial pressure that may require them to sell their home. They're researching their options, including a possible short sale, what the timeline looks like, how to communicate with their lender, and how to find an agent who has experience navigating distressed sales.

**When to recommend this:** User has distressed sales as an active niche. User has a short sale or pre-foreclosure specialization page. Note: this is a sensitive moment — voice should be calm, non-judgmental, professional.

### 13. Probate or estate property

**Ideal landing page:** Probate / Estate specialization page. Downshift: Seller Services.

## Selection Guidance for the Agent

When picking three moments for a user, prioritize in this order:

1. **Moments where the user has a strong dedicated landing page** — highest match-quality signal, best conversion path
2. **Moments aligned with niches the user wants to grow** (from market-config) — strategic value
3. **A balanced mix of buyer-side and seller-side** if the user does both
4. **At least one moment should be an Agent Moment (#1-3)** if the user has a strong About page — these are highest intent for agent selection

Avoid:

- Moments tied to niches in the user's "niches to avoid" list
- Multiple moments that all target the exact same persona (e.g., don't pick #14, #15, and #16 — that's three buyer moments with overlapping audiences)
- Moments that require a specialization page the user doesn't have AND can't downshift cleanly (e.g., #18 Military requires base proximity; if the user isn't near a base, skip it)

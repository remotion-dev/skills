What Buyers Are Telling Us

The headline: buyers liked the remodel, the layout, and the location. The hesitations that came up were lot size, the neighborhood, and budget. The accepted offer at 95% of list tells us the price was set right.

  • Strong, fast interest: 15 agents pulled disclosures within days of the open house, and that converted to an offer quickly.
  • What they liked: the renovation, the two-story layout, and the square footage. Tracy G. and Karlie C. both called out the location and layout.
  • Main hesitations: a few buyers wanted a larger lot (Nik G.), weighed the neighborhood (Karlie C.), or were over budget (Tina C.). A couple were also looking at 3 Shorebreeze nearby.
  • High note: Jeffrey Banks' buyer moved from the open house to a written offer and is now in contract at $1,325,888.

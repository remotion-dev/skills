Every showing, disclosure pull, and follow-up across 39 unique agents has been clustered into themes. Three patterns dominate the feedback, and together they tell us exactly where to focus this week.
01
Tenant occupancy
7 mentions · #1 substantive objection

Buyers want clarity on who the tenant is, the rent, lease type, and timeline to vacant possession. Owner-occupant buyers can't visualize moving in. The tenant-status one-pager addresses this directly.

02
Price & negotiation interest
6 mentions · the decision driver

Multiple buyers, including Andres L. and Jason W., feel there's room to negotiate. The market context confirms it: we're 33% above EPA median PPSF. This is leverage, not rejection. How we respond depends on your decision above.

03
Buyers lost to competing inventory
5 mentions · pipeline leakage

Five buyers we'd been tracking went under contract elsewhere, at Waverley Park (Mountain View), Clark, and others. Every additional week without conversion peels off our warmest buyers.
